📊 Smartphone Business Insights Dashboard
A Power BI Data Analytics Project

This Power BI dashboard provides a complete analysis of smartphone sales, customer behavior, and business performance across major mobile brands. It highlights key trends in sales, transactions, ratings, and payment methods, enabling data‑driven decision‑making.

🔍 Key Insights
Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Vivo, Xiaomi show strong and competitive sales performance

iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, Galaxy Note 20 are the top‑selling smartphone models

UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, Cash transactions are almost evenly distributed

Saturday and Monday record the highest sales volume

Customer satisfaction is high, with 5‑star ratings dominating

City‑wise sales distribution highlights major demand hubs across India


📁 Dataset Overview
The dataset includes the following fields:

Brand

Mobile Model

Quantity Sold

Total Sales

Transactions

Payment Method

Customer Rating

City

Order Date

These fields were cleaned, transformed, and modeled in Power BI to generate meaningful insights.


📈 Dashboard Features
KPI Cards: Total Sales, Quantity, Transactions, Average Sales

Brand‑wise Performance Table

Monthly & Daily Sales Trend Line Chart

City‑wise Units Sold (Map Visualization)

Model‑wise Sales Bar Chart

Payment Method Distribution (Pie Chart)

Customer Rating Distribution

Sales by Day of Week
